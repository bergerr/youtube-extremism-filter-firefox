Youtube Extremism Filter


Here you can manage the channels you want to block or allow. Channels in the blacklist will not be shown in the recommended videos.

To remove a channel from the blacklist, select it and click Whitelist selected.

To add a removed channel back to the blacklist, select it and click Blacklist selected.

You can also add channels to the blacklist by entering their names in the Custom channels section and clicking Save channels.

Once a channel has been blocked it won't be recommended, regardless of which list it is on.
